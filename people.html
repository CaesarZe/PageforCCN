Principal Investigator

Distinguished Professor, Faculty of Psychology, BNU
Pengfei Xu
Director, Computational Cognitive Neuroscience Group; Deputy Director, Beijing Key Laboratory of Applied Experimental Psychology.

PhD in Cognitive Neuroscience (Beijing Normal University) and PhD in Neuropsychiatry (University of Groningen). Research interests: neuroimaging, intracranial recordings, computational modeling, and cognitive techniques to investigate decision-making in emotional disorders.
