Research Overview

The Computational Cognitive Neuroscience Lab (CCNlab) investigates the neural and computational mechanisms underlying emotion, decision-making, and mental disorders. We integrate multi-modal neuroimaging (fMRI, fNIRS, sEEG), computational modeling, and clinical assessment to explore cross-diagnostic biomarkers and brain-behavior relationships.

  • Decision-making and emotional regulation in psychiatric populations
  • Neurocomputational models of affective and social cognition
  • Predictive modeling for clinical translation and personalized intervention
